Who we are

Natasha Frauendorf & Lynne van Coller are the owners of Serendipity - Storage for Small Spaces. They handcraft their storage cabinets from home and have been doing so since April of 2021. Transforming clutter into clean lines and creating beautiful storage spaces, they turn customers' under-utilized small spaces into pieces that bring joy.
