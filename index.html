About

Natasha Frauendorf & Lynne van Coller are the owners of Serendipity - Storage for Small Spaces. They handcraft their storage cabinets from home and have been doing so since April of 2021.

"This is why we named our business Serendipity - we found something beautiful without looking for it. And we are creating beautiful things for people who maybe just happen to see our ads."

Our passion is to create happy spaces that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. Our goal is to continue turing customers' small space into one that bring them joy!
